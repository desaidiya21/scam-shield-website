About Scam Shield

Scam Shield is a cybersecurity awareness website created to help people recognize, understand, and avoid online scams. Our goal is to educate users about common cyber threats and encourage safe internet practices.

The Scam Checker helps users analyze suspicious websites, phishing links, emails, and text messages to identify possible scam indicators before they become victims.

Scam Shield also provides useful cybersecurity tips about phishing attacks, fake shopping websites, lottery scams, investment fraud, banking scams, QR code scams, and social media scams. By understanding these threats, users can better protect their personal information and finances.

Our mission is to make the internet a safer place by promoting cybersecurity awareness, encouraging responsible online behavior, and helping people stay one step ahead of cybercriminals.
